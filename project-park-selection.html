Multi-Criteria Cartographic Modeling for Park Site Selection

Dec 2025

Technical Implementation

  • Built a constraint-based spatial decision model to identify suitable park sites by integrating land ownership, terrain slope, transportation access, hydrology, and wetlands restrictions.
  • Designed a cartographic modeling workflow using buffers, intersects, unions, and erases to enforce planning constraints.
  • Translated criteria into binary spatial masks and combined them through logical AND overlays to produce defensible suitability results.
  • Converted raster-derived slope classes to vector polygons, corrected multipart geometries, recalculated areas, and filtered candidates by minimum area thresholds.
