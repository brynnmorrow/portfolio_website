Map Projection Analysis & Coordinate System Management

Geospatial Analysis & Cartography | Sept 2025

Technical Implementation

  • Compared multiple map projections (Albers Equal Area, UTM Zone 15N, and Mercator) by measuring real-world distances and coordinates to demonstrate how projection choice effects spatial accuracy and distortion
  • Analyzed planar vs. geodesic distance calculations to measure projection caused error, highlighting how improper coordinate systems can create measurement inaccuracies.
  • Created multi-map layouts with inset views of different coordinate systems while managing reprojection and permanent reprojection workflows using ArcGIS Pro's Project and Define Projection tools.
