Raster-Based Cost Surface Modeling \& Suitability Analysis

Raster GIS & Spatial Modeling | Sept 2025

Technical Implementation

  • Constructed a multi-layer raster cost surface integrating terrain slope and distance to transportation networks, using mathematical raster algebra to model real-world access and construction costs.
  • Applied advanced raster operations, including resampling, mosaicking, low-pass filtering, conditional replacement, and permanent reclassification, to correct elevation artifacts while preserving fine-scale terrain detail.
  • Developed a threshold-based suitability mask to isolate areas meeting a fixed budget constraint, producing a multi-panel cartographic layout in order to communicate intermediate and final modeling results.
